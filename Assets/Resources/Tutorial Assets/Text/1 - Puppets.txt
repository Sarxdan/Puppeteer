As a <i>puppet</i>, your goal is to escape the mansion, either by yourself or as a team.
You may use weapons, abilities and teamplay to help navigate to the exit.

- Use <b>W/A/S/D</b> for movement and <b>SHIFT</b> for sprinting
- Press <b>E</b> to interact
- Press <b>F</b> to activate your special ability
